# Terms Of Service Draft

Status: draft only. External legal review is required before paid or public
launch.

## Service

wr3 provides AI-assisted smart-contract pre-audit, exploitability triage, risk
scoring, reporting, and optional monitoring. wr3 does not provide a guarantee of
security, insurance, legal advice, or a replacement for a human security audit.

## User Responsibilities

Users must only submit contracts, source code, addresses, and repositories that
they are authorized to analyze or that are available for passive public review.
Users must not request live exploitation, mainnet transactions, theft, market
manipulation, or disclosure of working exploit steps for third-party systems.

## Prohibited Use

- Active attacks outside explicit written authorization or Safe Harbor scope.
- Mainnet exploitation.
- Public accusation of fraud/scam based only on wr3 output.
- Attempts to extract private findings, raw outputs, PoCs, or customer source.
- Submitting malware or prompt-injection payloads to control the system.

## Reports

Reports are AI-assisted and may contain false positives or false negatives. Paid
reports include scope, limitations, engine version, score version, and reviewer
status where applicable.

## Liability

Draft position: liability is capped at the amount paid for the relevant audit or
subscription period. This clause requires legal review for enforceability.

## Refunds

Refund rules are defined in `docs/REFUND_POLICY.md`.

## Data Retention

Retention rules are defined in `docs/DATA_RETENTION_DELETION_POLICY.md`.

## Changes

wr3 may update scoring methodology, engines, pricing, and terms. Methodology
changes must be versioned and should not silently rewrite old report versions.
