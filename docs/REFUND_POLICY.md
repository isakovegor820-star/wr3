# Refund Policy Draft

Status: draft only. Legal and payment-provider review required.

## Subscriptions

Monthly subscription fees are generally non-refundable once scans or gated
artifacts have been used during the billing period.

## One-Shot Reports

Refund is available when:

- wr3 fails to deliver the agreed report within the stated SLA, and no revised
  delivery time was accepted by the client.
- The delivered report is technically empty because the wr3 system failed.
- wr3 accepted a target that should have been rejected as unsupported and no
  useful fallback report was delivered.

Refund is not available solely because:

- wr3 did not find a vulnerability.
- A later human auditor found a vulnerability that wr3 missed.
- A finding is disputed but the report contains reasonable evidence and
  limitations.

## Disputes

Disputed High/Critical findings should receive human review before refund
decision. The review should check affected code, severity, impact wording,
recommendation, and scope.

## Provider Fees

Payment provider fees and network gas may be non-refundable depending on the
provider. This must be disclosed at checkout.
