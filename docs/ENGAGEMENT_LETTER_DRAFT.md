# Engagement Letter / Statement Of Work Draft

Status: draft only. External legal review is required before first paid audit.

## Parties

- Client: to be filled per engagement.
- wr3: to be filled after legal entity or bootstrap contracting decision.

## Scope

The engagement covers AI-assisted pre-audit and exploitability triage for the
contracts, addresses, repositories, chains, and commits listed in the order.

Out of scope unless explicitly written:

- Mainnet transactions.
- Active exploitation.
- Social engineering.
- Infrastructure penetration testing.
- Cyber insurance.
- Public claims about third-party projects.

## Deliverables

- HTML/Markdown report.
- Score and score breakdown.
- Findings with severity, confidence, exploitability, evidence, and next steps.
- PoC artifact or failure reason for eligible High/Critical findings when the
  tier/package includes PoC attempts.
- Optional human review status for paid/public High/Critical claims.

## Authorization

Client confirms they are authorized to submit the target systems for analysis
and to receive the resulting findings.

## Limitations

wr3 output is not a guarantee that vulnerabilities do not exist. AI and static
tools can miss vulnerabilities or report false positives.

## Responsible Disclosure

If wr3 discovers a sensitive third-party issue during authorized work, wr3 and
client will follow the responsible disclosure policy unless the parties agree in
writing to a stricter process.

## Acceptance

Client accepts delivery when the report is accessible through the owner-gated
report URL or delivered through an agreed private channel.
