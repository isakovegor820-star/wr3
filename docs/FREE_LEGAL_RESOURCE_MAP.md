## Non-Negotiable Blocker

Before paid/public launch, an external legal reviewer must check:

- liability cap enforceability
- AI-assisted audit disclaimers
- data retention/deletion commitments
- responsible disclosure windows
- payment/refund terms
- jurisdiction/tax assumptions
- wording around third-party public project pages

## Safe Default

Until legal review is complete:

- Private reports only.
- Public High/Critical claims off.
- No active mainnet actions.
- No "scam/fraud" wording.
- No promise of complete audit coverage.
