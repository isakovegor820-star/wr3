# AI-Assisted Audit Disclaimer

wr3 reports are AI-assisted smart-contract pre-audits and exploitability triage.
They are not a guarantee of security and are not a replacement for a full human
security audit.

wr3 may use static analysis, deterministic heuristics, LLM triage, local fork or
test-validator PoC attempts, fuzzing, and human review depending on the selected
tier and scope. These methods can miss vulnerabilities and can produce false
positives.

Unless a report explicitly says otherwise:

- No mainnet transaction was performed.
- No active exploitation was performed.
- PoC artifacts are isolated, private, and scoped.
- Public High/Critical claims require human approval.
- The score applies only to the analyzed input, engine version, score version,
  timestamp, and available source/metadata.

Suggested report wording:

> This is an AI-assisted pre-audit and exploitability triage report generated by
> wr3. It is not a guarantee that the contract is secure and should not replace
> human review for production or high-value deployments.
